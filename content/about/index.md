## Who I am

I'm an experienced Cloud Solutions Architect with over **28 years in the IT industry**, designing, educating, and building complex cloud solutions for a diverse set of enterprise customers. My career has spanned roles as Cloud Solutions Architect, Technical Trainer, Customer Success Manager, Sales Consultant, and Developer.

Today, as a **Senior Cloud Solution Architect in Microsoft's Cloud + AI organization**, I specialize in **Azure AI Foundry**, partnering with enterprise manufacturing customers to design and deliver AI-driven, cloud-native solutions that accelerate digital transformation.

I'm fluent in **English** and **Portuguese**, and based in the Orlando, FL area.

## What I do

- Design secure, scalable cloud and AI application architectures on Microsoft Azure

- Drive DevSecOps adoption with GitHub, Azure DevOps, and CI/CD pipelines

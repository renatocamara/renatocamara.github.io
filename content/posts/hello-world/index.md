Welcome to my corner of the internet. This site is a professional technical portfolio — skills, experience, certifications, and projects — plus a blog on Azure, AI, and cloud architecture.

The stack, for the curious:

- **Hugo** static site generator with the **Blowfish** theme, managed as a **Hugo Module**
- **GitHub Actions** builds and deploys on every push
- **GitHub Pages** hosting with the custom domain **renatocamara.net**

Every deployment is just `git push`. More posts coming soon.
